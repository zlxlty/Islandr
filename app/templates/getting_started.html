Getting Started

Islandr platform has three main functions:

1. For students to explore and find activities they like conveniently.
2. For organizers to create and publicize their activities.
3. For The Event Committee to manage and supervise all activities.

Please read the following instructions to learn how to use Islandr platform.

Create Account

Registration

To create your own account, click `log in` button on home page and then click `Click Here` to register

You can use any username you like, but please use your UWC email address to register.

After registration, a confirmation email will be sent to you. Due to some unknown problems, the email will likely be in your trash can. Please click the confirmation links in that email and your account will be confirmed.

After confirmation, you are ready to use Islandr platform

Complete Profile

Many information about your account is not completed when you just register. In order for other user to know you better and contact you more conveniently, you can edit your account in My Account page.

For Everyone

This section is for all the users to familarize the main functions of Islandr

Tag System

In order for you to find the event that you are interested in, we categorized all the events into six tags --- Arts, Culture, Environment, Music, Academic and Sports.

When `click an icon`, you will go to the page where the events filtered by the tag you choose. For example, if you click `Arts`, you will go to the page below:

Search System

On the top right corner, a search bar is designed to search for any event, user or teams.

Calendar System

In the home page below the ‘tag system’, there is a Calendar for the current week. You can click the weekdays on the bar to check the events at that day.

Also, if you click the View All button on the top right corner, you will see all the events on the website.

Weekly Bulletin

All registrated users will receive a weekly bulletin email in the morning on Sunday. This email will inform you about all the events that will happen next week from Monday to Sunday. You can click the ‘learn more’ button to visit Islandr for details.

Attend Events

After you find the event you like, you can click the ‘attend’ button at the bottom-right corner on the event page to become one of the attendants

You can also view all the attendants for this event by clicking the profile picture next to the ‘Attend’ button.

You can see all the events you have attended in ‘my account’ page

Email Reminder

After attending one event on Islandr, we will send you a reminder email a day before that event happens so that you won’t forget to go to the event.

Explore Team

Except from showing events, demonstrating teams is another main function of Islandr platform. Teams with different scales can present their ideas, organize their own events and recruit student that they need. As a normal student, you can also search for teams that you are interested in. On the home page, different teams will be pushed to you in three distinct ways --- latest, popular and random.

If you can’t find any interesting teams, you can also click ‘View All’ to see all the teams.

Join Teams

After you find a team that fits you, you can hit the ‘join’ button to apply to the team.

The team leader will decide whether to accept you or not. If accepted, you will become one of the official members in that team.

Message

You can see all of your messages by clicking the ‘Message’ button.

Moment

To visit the moment page, you can click ‘Moments’ on the navigate bar.

In the moment page, you will see the moments posted by the Teams. Click the profile photo to visit the Team profile.

Also, you can visit the event by click the name of the envent.

Moreover, if you think an event is good, you can click the like button at the down right corner of a moment to like an event.

For Team Leader

Create A Team

To create a team, click the Team button on the navigate bar and click Create Team.

And you will see the Create Team page. Click ‘Download: Team Proposal Template’ to download the template, complete it and click ‘select file’ button to upload the proposal.

Remember to fill in all the blank. After you finished, click ‘submit’ to create a team

The below picture is an example Team profile. Also, you can click ‘Edit Profit’ to change any information.

Post An Event

Note that only team leader can post the event. To create an event, first go to the Team profile page, then click New Event on the down right corner of the profile part.

Here, you need to fill out all the information to create an event. In the post, to up load the picture, you should click ‘Upload Local Image’ at the bottom. When you click the bottom, you will go to a website.

Then, choose the image you want to upload

After you up load an image, you need to click the link to go to your picture.

Then, right click the image and click ‘copy image link’.

Then, go back to Islandr.xyz, Click ‘insert’ and then click ‘image’.

Finally, paste the link of the picture in Source, fill the blank and click ‘Save’. (you can leave width and height blank)

Post A Moment

Only a leader of a group can post moments and each moment has to related to an event that this group organized.

Go to your team and click New Moment to post a moment.

Group leaders can upload up to eight pictures to record the best moment in their events and attract other students.
